Privacy Policy

Effective date: 28 April 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Captura Android application (the "App"), published by Shahu Studio (the "Developer"). The App is offered as an ad-supported service with an optional one-time Pro upgrade that removes ads.

1. The short version

Captura is designed so that your captures stay yours. Screen recordings, screenshots, and microphone audio that the App records are saved only on your device. The Developer does not operate any server, backend, or cloud account. Your captures never leave your phone unless you choose to share them through another app.

2. What the App accesses on your device (but does not transmit)

These items stay on your device and are never sent to the Developer or any third party:

  • Screen content — recorded only while you have an active recording session that you started, and saved to your device's local storage as a video file.
  • Screenshots — captured only when you press the screenshot button or trigger a shake gesture, and saved to your device's local storage as an image file.
  • Microphone audio — captured only when you enable the microphone toggle inside the App, and muxed directly into the local video file.
  • Front camera (facecam) — accessed only when you enable the facecam overlay, displayed as an on-screen bubble, and recorded into the local video file.

None of the above is uploaded, streamed, transmitted, or shared with the Developer or any third party. You can stop the App from accessing any of these by disabling the relevant toggle inside the App, by revoking the permission in your device settings, or by uninstalling the App.

3. What is collected and transmitted

The App uses Google's Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) to display ads, and Google Play Billing to process the optional Pro upgrade. Through these Google services, the following data may be collected and transmitted to Google:

  • Advertising ID — a resettable identifier provided by your device, used by AdMob for advertising and analytics. You can reset or limit this from your device's Google settings.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics — automatically collected by the AdMob SDK to help Google diagnose ad-related issues.
  • Purchase history — when you buy or restore the Pro upgrade, Google Play Billing records the purchase against your Google account so the App can verify your entitlement.

The Developer does not receive personally identifiable information about you through these flows. Aggregated, non-identifying ad performance reports may be available to the Developer through the AdMob console.

4. Permissions the App requests, and why

  • Camera — only if you enable the facecam overlay.
  • Microphone — only if you enable mic recording.
  • Notifications — to show capture controls and saved-file alerts.
  • Display over other apps (overlay) — for the floating capture button and the facecam bubble.
  • Media access — to read your saved Captura recordings and screenshots back into the in-app Gallery.
  • Screen recording (MediaProjection) — granted per session by Android, on your explicit consent each time recording starts.

5. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. The Developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the App in a way that resulted in personal information being shared with the Developer, please contact the Developer at the email below and the data will be removed.

6. Data retention and deletion

The Developer does not store any of your data on a server. Captures saved by the App live in your device's local storage, under your control, and are deleted when you delete them or uninstall the App. Advertising ID and related ad-tech data held by Google are subject to Google's own retention policies and your device's ad-ID controls.

If you wish to request deletion of any data the Developer may hold in connection with the App, contact shahustudioco@gmail.com and the request will be actioned within 30 days. Because the Developer does not operate a backend, in practice this means confirming what data, if any, exists.

7. Third-party services

The App relies on the following Google services. Their own privacy policies govern any data they collect:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing

8. Security

Data transmitted by the AdMob and Play Billing SDKs is encrypted in transit via standard HTTPS. The App itself does not transmit your captures or personal data. As with any mobile software, no method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure; please use the App with the same care you would apply to any other app on your phone.

9. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. When it changes, the Developer will update this page and revise the effective date at the top. Continued use of the App after a change indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your consent

By installing or continuing to use Captura, you consent to the practices described in this privacy policy. If you live in a region with regional privacy laws (e.g. GDPR in the EU/UK), you may be presented with a consent dialog the first time the App requests an ad; you can change your choice later from inside the App or your device settings.

11. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy or the App's data practices can be sent to: shahustudioco@gmail.com.
